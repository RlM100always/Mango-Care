About the Project

MangoCare is a comprehensive platform developed as part of the S125 HIT237 Building Interactive Software course. Our mission is to empower farmers and agricultural professionals with cutting-edge tools and knowledge for effective mango pest and disease management.

Through our platform, users can access detailed information about common mango pests and diseases, including identification guides, symptoms, and treatment recommendations. We've combined technical expertise with user-friendly design to make this information accessible to everyone.

Our team is committed to continuous improvement and innovation in agricultural technology, ensuring that MangoCare remains at the forefront of digital solutions for mango farming.
